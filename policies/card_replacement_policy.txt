Card Replacement and Lost/Stolen Card Policy

If a debit or credit card is lost or stolen, the customer should report it
immediately so the card can be deactivated and fraudulent use prevented.
Once a card is reported lost or stolen, it cannot be reactivated -- a new
card with a new number must be issued.

Standard replacement cards arrive by mail within 7-10 business days at no
charge. Expedited shipping is available for a $25 fee and arrives within
2 business days.

Customers are not liable for unauthorized transactions made after a card is
reported lost or stolen, provided the report is made promptly. Liability
for transactions made before the report depends on how quickly the loss was
reported, consistent with Regulation E.
