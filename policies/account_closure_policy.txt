Account Closure Policy

Customers may close a checking or savings account at any time by visiting
a branch, calling support, or submitting a request through the mobile app,
provided the account balance is zero or positive. Any remaining positive
balance is issued as a check or transferred to another account within 5
business days of closure.

Accounts with a negative balance cannot be closed until the balance is
resolved. Accounts inactive for 12 months or more (no customer-initiated
transactions) may be classified as dormant; dormant accounts inactive for 3
years or more may be subject to state escheatment laws and the remaining
balance transferred to the state's unclaimed property division.

Closing an account does not affect a customer's credit score, since deposit
accounts are not reported to credit bureaus.
