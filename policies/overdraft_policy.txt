Overdraft Policy

If a customer's checking account balance goes negative due to a transaction,
a standard overdraft fee of $34 is charged per item, up to a maximum of 3
fees per business day ($102 maximum daily total). Overdraft fees are not
charged on transactions of $5 or less, or if the account is overdrawn by
$5 or less at the end of the business day.

Customers enrolled in Overdraft Protection can link a savings account or
credit card to automatically cover a shortfall instead of paying a fee. It
takes 1 business day to enroll in Overdraft Protection after a request is
submitted.

Accounts that remain overdrawn for more than 30 consecutive days may be
closed and referred to collections. Customers can request a one-time
courtesy fee refund once every 12 months by contacting support.
